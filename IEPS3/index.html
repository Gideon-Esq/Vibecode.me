About IEPS 3.0

A flagship academic summit organized by the Education Students' Representative Council (ESRC) of Obafemi Awolowo University.

Welcome to the 3rd edition of the Ife Education Parliamentary Summit. This gathering serves as a critical nexus where academic brilliance meets legislative foresight. By bringing together students, academics, policymakers, and legislators, we aim to forge actionable pathways for educational development in Nigeria.

This year's theme focuses on the pivotal role of Nigerian Parliamentarians in driving educational reforms, addressing funding gaps, and ultimately contributing to holistic nation-building.
